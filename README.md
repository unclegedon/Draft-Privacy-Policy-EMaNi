# Privacy Policy for EMaNi

**Last Updated:** February 17, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **EMaNi**. This privacy policy explains how we handle user information for our application, which serves as a mobile extension of our ERP system.

## 2. Information We Collect
To provide a functional experience, EMaNi may collect:
* **Account Information:** Email and name used for ERP system authentication.
* **Location Data:** We may collect precise or approximate location data to verify salesman activities and visit logs.
* **Device Information:** Device model and OS version for technical support and performance monitoring.

## 3. How We Use Your Data
The data collected is used solely for:
* Authenticating users into the ERP environment.
* Tracking field activities to ensure data integrity in the ERP system.
* Improving application stability.

## 4. Data Security
We prioritize the security of your data. Information is transmitted over secure protocols and stored within our private database. We do not share or sell your data to third parties.

## 5. User Permissions
You can manage permissions (Camera, Location, Storage) through your device settings at any time.
